India's Healthcare Emergency

  • 2.4 million preventable deaths annually due to fragmented health systems
  • 70% of medical errors occur during critical emergency response periods
  • COVID-19 revealed systemic failures - inadequate early warning and resource allocation
  • Paper-based records create insurmountable barriers to effective care

The cost of inaction: Lives lost, families devastated

Current Healthcare Limitations

  • Fragmented Data Storage - No unified patient identification system
  • Delayed Outbreak Detection - Traditional surveillance methods insufficient
  • Emergency Response Failures - 45+ minutes for medical history access
  • Digital Infrastructure Underutilized - ABDM and Aadhaar systems disconnected

Opportunity: Transform existing infrastructure into lifesaving platform

Revolutionizing Indian Healthcare
